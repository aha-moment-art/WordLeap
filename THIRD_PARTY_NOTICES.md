# Third-party data

WordLeap includes vocabulary data derived from [ECDICT](https://github.com/skywind3000/ECDICT), the Free English to Chinese Dictionary Database.

ECDICT is distributed under the MIT License. Copyright (c) 2017 skywind3000.
The original license text is preserved in `THIRD_PARTY_LICENSES/ECDICT-LICENSE`.

The PTE vocabulary data is derived from the PTE junior and senior dictionaries in [Qwerty Learner](https://github.com/RealKai42/qwerty-learner), distributed under GPL-3.0. The derived PTE JSON remains a separate GPL-3.0 data work; the derived JSON and reproducible importer are included in this repository. The license text is preserved in `THIRD_PARTY_LICENSES/QWERTY-LEARNER-GPL-3.0`.
